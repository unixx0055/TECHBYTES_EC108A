Anti-Ragging

At the Academy of Technology, we are resolute in our commitment to maintaining a campus
environment that is entirely free from the detrimental practice of ragging. To reinforce this
commitment, we have established a robust Anti-Ragging Committee that diligently oversees and
regulates campus activities. This committee is proactive in ensuring the strict adherence to our anti-ragging
policies. Regular inspections and monitoring are conducted to identify and address any
potential instances promptly. In the rare event that an issue arises, the Anti-Ragging Committee
takes swift and decisive action, implementing proper measures to eradicate any such behavior and
provide a secure atmosphere for all students. This stringent approach reflects our dedication
fostering a campus culture characterized by mutual respect, collaboration, and a genuine focus
on academic and personal growth.
